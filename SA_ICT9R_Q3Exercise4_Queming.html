What is Manila zoo and Why do I love it?


Manila Zoo is a popular zoological park located in the heart of Manila, Philippines. It features a diverse collection of animal species from around the world, including tigers, elephants, giraffes, and many more animals, I love manila zoo because it was a place where my mom and grandfather would take me occasionally when I was very young and I think it is one of the main reasons why I became an animal lover today,sadly over the years manila zoo is no longer in its former glory most animals now looks pityful and are not taken care of properly.

to do's in Manila Zoo

Visitors to Manila Zoo can enjoy a variety of activities and attractions. They can explore the animal exhibits, including the newly-renovated Reptile House and the aviary with over 100 bird species. The zoo also offers animal encounters where visitors can interact with some of the animals like the pythons and birds of prey. Additionally, guests can take a relaxing stroll through the botanical gardens and the children's zoo area which features a petting zoo and a playground.
